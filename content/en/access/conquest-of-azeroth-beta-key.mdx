<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Setting Up Your Client and Redeeming Access">

Once you have secured your key or purchased a bundle, you must configure the official Project Ascension launcher to log in and play. Follow these steps to set up your client:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create Your Ascension Account">
    Visit the official [Project Ascension Website](https://ascension.gg/en) and register a single master account using your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download the Official Launcher">
    Navigate to the download portal and install the official Windows launcher. Let the launcher fully patch your base client to avoid interface errors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Your Beta Key or Bundle">
    If you received a beta key from a giveaway, redeem it on your account dashboard. If you bought a shop bundle, verify that the purchase shows as active in your account transaction history.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Select the Correct Realm Client">
    Open the launcher dropdown menu located above the 'Play' button. For closed beta testing, select **PTR**. For the full release on July 3, 2026, select the **Conquest of Azeroth** realm path (Vol'Jin).
  </Step>
</Steps>
