<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Modes and High Risk Systems">

The PvP landscape in **Conquest of Azeroth free** is divided into three distinct phases to cater to different player preferences. Unlike standard servers where PvP is often an afterthought, CoA integrates it into the world through the Hybrid Risk System. Players can choose to remain in a safe PvE phase, enter a standard PvP phase, or brave the High Risk phase where the stakes are significantly higher. In High Risk, defeated players drop chests containing a portion of their equipped gear and inventory, creating a tense, high-stakes environment.

<Callout type="info" title="High Risk Rewards">
High Risk players have access to 'Bloodforged' versions of dungeon and raid gear, which can be found from open-world monsters or looted from other players.
</Callout>

| PvP Mode | Risk Level | Death Penalty | Unique Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PvE Mode** | Low | None | Safe exploration and dungeon grinding |
| **PvP Mode** | Medium | Durability Loss | Open-world combat and PvP reputation |
| **High Risk** | High | **Gear Drop** | Bloodforged items and enemy loot |

**Key PvP Features:**
- **Arenas**: 1v1, 2v2, and 3v3 solo queue formats at level 60.
- **Battlegrounds**: Objective-based team combat for PvP progression.
- **World Events**: Crow's Cache (Guild-vs-Guild) and the Gurubashi Blood Bowl.
- **CC Identity**: Classes have thematic control tools (e.g., Necromancer Fear) rather than generic stuns.
