<Section icon={Zap} title="Talent Trees and Build Optimization">

The talent system in Conquest of Azeroth is inspired by the modern Dragonflight design. Upon reaching **Level 10**, you must select a primary specialization. This choice unlocks two distinct trees: the **Class Tree** (utility and shared tools) and the **Specialization Tree** (role-specific power).

| Build Type | Class | Focus | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Demolition** | Tinker | Explosives & Rockets | Ranged Burst DPS |
| **Influence** | Cultist | Shields & Dark Heals | Support / Healing |
| **Incineration** | Pyromancer | Fire DoTs & Meteors | AoE Damage |
| **Archery** | Ranger | Quiver Skills & Mobility | Physical Ranged |
| **Warden** | Starcaller | Lunar Barriers & Tanking | Mitigation Tanking |

Points alternate between these two trees as you level up, preventing you from over-investing in one area too early. This ensures a balanced power curve where you gain both new utility and increased throughput simultaneously.

For players new to the **conquest of azeroth alpha bundle**, the **Auto-Build** feature is highly recommended. It automatically allocates your points into a viable configuration, allowing you to focus on learning the combat rotation first.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame PvE and PvP Systems">

Once you reach the level cap, the **conquest of azeroth alpha bundle** content expands into high-tier PvE and competitive PvP. The PvE endgame focuses on Vanilla-era dungeons and raids but tuned specifically for the power levels of the 21 new classes. This includes scaling difficulties like **Mythic+** for 5-man groups and **Ascended** raids for large teams.

On the PvP side, the project utilizes a **Hybrid Risk System**. Players can choose their level of engagement:

1.  **PvE Mode**: Low risk, no open-world ganking.
2.  **PvP Mode**: Medium risk, open-world combat enabled.
3.  **High Risk PvP**: A separate phase where players drop chests containing gear upon death.

| Mode | Risk Level | Primary Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Dungeons** | Low | Entry-level gear |
| **Mythic+** | Scaling | Top-tier 5-man loot |
| **Ascended Raids** | High | Legendary items / Tier sets |
| **High Risk PvP** | Extreme | Bloodforged gear / Enemy loot |

<Callout type="warning" title="High Risk Warning">
Entering the High Risk PvP phase is permanent for that session. Ensure your build is optimized for 1v1 encounters before stepping into this phase, as you will likely face specialized PvP builds like the **Witch Hunter** or **Felsworn**.
</Callout>
