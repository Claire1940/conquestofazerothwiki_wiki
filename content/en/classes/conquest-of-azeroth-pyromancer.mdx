<Section icon={Shield} title="Flame Weaver: The Path of Renewing Flame">

The Flame Weaver is the healing branch of the **conquest of azeroth pyromancer**. They wield the strength of the Phoenix's renewing flame to rekindle the fighting spirit of their allies. This spec is ideal for players who enjoy "fist-weaving" or "atonement" style gameplay, where dealing damage directly contributes to the survival of the group.

Flame Weavers restore allies using a combination of healing-over-time (HoT) effects and blasts of cauterizing fire. Their mythical phoenix companion assists in casting, often duplicating or enhancing the healer's spells to ensure the party stays healthy during intense combat.

| Key Healing Spell | Effect Description | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phoenix Flame** | Mends the party with a burst of life | Emergency group healing |
| **Renewing Flame** | A powerful HoT that scales with Crit | Sustained tank maintenance |
| **Cauterizing Fire** | Cleanses wounds while dealing minor damage | Single target spot healing |
| **Embrace of the Phoenix** | Resurrects a fallen ally mid-combat | Recovery after a wipe |

<Callout type="success" title="Healing Strategy">
As a Flame Weaver, never stop casting damage spells. Your passive abilities convert a portion of your fire damage into smart-healing for nearby allies, allowing you to maintain pressure on the boss while keeping health bars full.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Flame} title="Incineration: Master of All-Consuming Flame">

If your goal is to reduce everything in your path to cinders, the Incineration spec is your best choice. These pyromancers are incarnations of destruction, wielding an array of damage-over-time effects and hard-hitting area-of-effect (AoE) spells.

The pinnacle of this spec is the **Fire Lord transformation**. Upon entering this state, the Pyromancer's spells are greatly enhanced, and they leave a trail of destruction that few enemies can survive.

**Key Spells in the Incineration Arsenal:**
- **Comet Storm**: Calls down icy-hot fragments to pummel a target area.
- **Firefall**: A massive meteor strike that deals heavy split damage.
- **Pillar of Flame**: Erupts underneath the enemy, launching smaller targets into the air.
- **Ignite**: A core DoT that spreads to nearby enemies when it crits.

| Damage Spell | Cast Time | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fireball** | 2.5s (Base) | Core builder, high crit synergy |
| **Comet Storm** | Instant | Best-in-class AoE burst |
| **Pillar of Flame** | 1.5s | Disrupts enemy casting and movement |
| **Fire Lord Form** | Instant | 20% Damage boost and new abilities |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mobility Warning">
Incineration pyromancers have long cast times. Position yourself carefully before starting your rotation, as moving frequently will significantly drop your DPS output. Use Pillar of Flame to buy yourself time.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Draconic: Agile Dragon Transformation">

The Draconic spec is the most unique path for the **conquest of azeroth pyromancer**. These pyromancers transform into Dracthyr, allowing them to soar into close-range spellcasting combat. They function as cunning assassins, using their flames to enhance their dragon-like forms and hunt down prey with unmatched agility.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Transform into Dracthyr">
    Activate your draconic form to gain access to Breath charges and enhanced physical resistance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Generate Breath Charges">
    Use agile movements and "Tail Swipe" or "Claw" attacks to build up your resource bar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Breath Attacks">
    Spend charges on devastating fire breath attacks that char enemies in a wide cone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Flight">
    Take to the skies for short periods to reposition or chase down escaping enemies in PvP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Draconic Mobility">
Unlike the other two specs, Draconic pyromancers are highly mobile. Their "Soar" ability allows them to bypass ground obstacles and attack from angles that stationary casters cannot reach.
</Callout>
