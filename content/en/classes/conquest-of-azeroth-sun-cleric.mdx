Unlike traditional classes that rely on a single resource, the Sun Cleric utilizes a combination of Mana, Rage, and a unique stacking buff known as Solar Power. As you perform combat actions and use your class abilities, you generate stacks of Solar Power. Once your pool of light is fully charged, you can unleash Dawn. Activating Dawn empowers your next five abilities with extra radiant effects, such as increased holy damage, bonus healing, or guaranteed critical strikes.

| Resource | Generation Method | Primary Use | Associated Specs |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mana** | Passive regeneration, talents | Spells, healing, utility | Blessings, Piety |
| **Rage** | Melee attacks, specific spells | Blazing attacks, heavy strikes | Valkyrie, Piety, Seraphim |
| **Solar Power** | Class ability execution | Activating Dawn empowerment | All Specializations |

<Callout type="tip" title="Solar Power Management">
Do not waste your Dawn empowerment on low-impact abilities. Plan your rotation so that your highest-damage or highest-healing spells benefit from the guaranteed critical strikes and radiant bonuses.
</Callout>

Each specialization alters how the Sun Cleric interacts with the enemy and manages resources. For example, Piety clerics function as war casters, using spells like Horath Blast to generate Rage, which is then spent on powerful Raptures. Valkyries, on the other hand, rely on physical attacks to build Daybreak stacks, allowing them to cast spells instantly mid-combat without interrupting their melee flow.

| Specialization | Role | Key Weaponry | Core Mechanic | Playstyle |
|---|---|---|---|---|

<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Rotation and Build Steps">

To maximize the efficiency of your Sun Cleric, you must follow a structured approach to combat. The rotation changes based on your chosen spec, but the fundamental goal remains the same: generate Solar Power, activate Dawn, and execute your empowered abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Generate Initial Resources">
    Start the fight by using your primary resource generators. For Piety, cast Horath Blast to build Rage. For Valkyrie, engage in melee combat to stack Daybreak.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stack Solar Power">
    Continue using your rotational abilities to build up your Solar Power pool. Watch the buff tracker to monitor when your pool is close to full.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash the Dawn">
    Trigger the Dawn ability once your Solar Power is maximized. This will buff your next five spells or attacks with radiant properties.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Empowered Spells">
    Cast your heavy-hitting abilities, such as Solar Wrath for DPS or massive heals for Blessings, to utilize the guaranteed critical strikes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When gearing your character, prioritizing the correct stats is essential for optimizing performance. The table below outlines the recommended stat priorities for each of the four paths.

| Specialization | Primary Stat | Secondary Stat | Tertiary Stat |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Piety** | Intellect | Haste | Spell Critical Strike |
| **Valkyrie** | Strength | Melee Critical Strike | Haste |
| **Seraphim** | Strength | Armor / Stamina | Parry / Dodge |
| **Blessings** | Intellect | Spell Power | Mana Regeneration |

<Callout type="success" title="Gear Selection Tip">
Look for gear that offers hybrid stats if you plan to swap specializations frequently. Strength-based gear with armor works well for both Valkyrie and Seraphim.
</Callout>
