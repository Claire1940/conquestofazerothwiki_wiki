<Section icon={Target} title="Key Active Abilities & Mechanics">

To play the **conquest of azeroth reaper** effectively, you must understand how its abilities interact. The core loop revolves around generating mana, reaping souls, and spending those souls to unleash empowered attacks [OCaUxzQg8dE].

### The Soul Harvesting Loop
1. **Reap**: Your primary melee attack. It deals normalized weapon damage and restores 5% of your maximum health [OCaUxzQg8dE]. The second consecutive use cleaves nearby enemies, and the third consecutive use grants you one **Reaped Soul** [OCaUxzQg8dE].
2. **Murder**: A 40-yard range instant cast that sends a soul crow to strike the target and restore 10% of your maximum mana [OCaUxzQg8dE]. Use this off cooldown to sustain your mana pool [OCaUxzQg8dE].
3. **Soul Infusion**: Once you collect three Reaped Souls, you become **Soul Infused** [OCaUxzQg8dE]. This state visually surrounds your character in purple energy and significantly upgrades specific abilities, such as turning Shutter Scythe from a 5-strike channel into a rapid 10-strike flurry [OCaUxzQg8dE].

<Section icon={Map} title="Leveling Progression & Rotation Guide">

Leveling a Reaper is a highly satisfying experience due to the class's built-in self-healing and crowd control [OCaUxzQg8dE]. By utilizing a specific rotation, you can pull multiple mobs at once without dropping low on health or running out of mana [OCaUxzQg8dE].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate and Control">
    Start the fight from range using **Soul Shock** to put the target to sleep for up to 30 seconds [OCaUxzQg8dE]. Your first attack against this sleeping "husk" deals 100% increased damage [OCaUxzQg8dE].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Burst Opener">
    Approach the sleeping target and open with **Dirge** to land a massive physical strike and immediately gain your first Reaped Soul [OCaUxzQg8dE].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Soul Generation Loop">
    Spam **Reap** to build up to three Reaped Souls, triggering your **Soul Infusion** state [OCaUxzQg8dE]. Use **Murder** whenever it is off cooldown to keep your mana topped off [OCaUxzQg8dE].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Empowered Execution">
    With Soul Infusion active, unleash **Shutter Scythe** to deliver 10 rapid strikes, melting the target's health bar while rapidly restoring your own HP [OCaUxzQg8dE].
  </Step>
</Steps>

For groups of enemies, modify your strategy by using **Blood Siphon** early in the pull to damage and heal from multiple targets simultaneously, followed by spamming the second-cast cleave mechanic of **Reap** [OCaUxzQg8dE].

<Callout type="info" title="Utility Tools">
Do not forget your utility spells. **Ghost Claw** allows you to steal 40% of an enemy's movement speed, making it impossible for them to kite you [OCaUxzQg8dE]. If you need to escape or position yourself, use **Underwalk** to enter the spirit realm and stealth past danger [OCaUxzQg8dE].
</Callout>
