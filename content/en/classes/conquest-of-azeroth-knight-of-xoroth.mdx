### Core Class Mechanics

The class relies heavily on managing two distinct resources: Rage and Deathfire [5]. You generate Rage through basic physical attacks and taking damage, then utilize that Rage to build up stacks of Deathfire [5]. Once your Deathfire stacks are high, you unleash them via powerful finishing spells that scale directly with the number of stacks consumed [5].

| Resource | Acquisition Method | Primary Usage |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Rage** | Auto-attacks, taking damage, specific physical strikes | Spent to generate Deathfire stacks and fuel basic abilities |
| **Deathfire** | Generated by spending Rage on core class abilities | Expended to cast devastating apocalyptic finishing moves |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always pool your Deathfire stacks to maximum capacity before casting your primary finishing spells. This ensures you bypass enemy resistances and maximize your burst potential [5].
</Callout>
