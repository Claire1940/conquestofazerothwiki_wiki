To master the **conquest of azeroth felsworn**, you must understand the unique rotation and mechanical flow of your chosen specialization [7mcE4UtAxNs].

### 1. Infernal: The Ranged Spell-Weaver
Infernals play like chaotic mages, corrupting and enfeebling their targets before obliterating them with high-damage projectile spells [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn]. Cunning and crowd control are your greatest weapons [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].

- **Control First**: Keep distance by trapping enemies using *Fel Prison* or stunning them with *Engulf* [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
- **Apply Afflictions**: Corrupt your targets to increase the damage of your subsequent direct-damage spells [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
- **Unleash Chaos**: Spend your generated Fel Fury on devastating *Fel Fireballs* and *Lances of Demonic Energy* [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].

### 2. Slayer: The Swift Dual-Wielder
Slayers are agile hunters who get up close and personal, relying on lightning-fast combos to trigger passive bursts of flame [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].

- **Mobility is Key**: Use *Fel Hoof Charge* to quickly close the gap or escape dangerous situations [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
- **Combo Building**: Chain your melee strikes in the correct sequence to trigger passive *Fel Strikes*, causing massive bursts of fire on all nearby enemies [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
- **Defensive Windows**: Activate *Idan's Guard* to grant yourself temporary invulnerability and vanish back into the shadows when targeted [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].

### 3. Tyrant: The Mind-Gaming Evasion Tank
Tyrants are unique tanks that turn defense into offense [7mcE4UtAxNs]. Instead of absorbing hits with a massive shield, they dodge incoming attacks to trigger powerful retaliatory strikes [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].

- **Dodge & Retaliate**: High dodge rating triggers automatic *Fel Strikes* whenever you evade an attack or activate *Vengeance* [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
- **Group Buffing**: Use *Infernal Whip Crack* to whip your allies into shape, boosting their haste while gaining personal combat bonuses based on the teammates you enrage [7mcE4UtAxNs, Felsworn].
