### Core Mechanic: The Loa and The Ward
The fundamental mechanic of the Witch Doctor involves balancing your active wards and Loa summons. Wards provide static benefits to an area, while Loa spirits can be called upon to provide temporary bursts of power or defensive utility.

| Specialization | Primary Mechanic | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Voodoo** | Insidious DoTs and Hexes | Solo Leveling / PvP Pressure |
| **Brewing** | Potion Tossing / Ward Placement | Dungeon Healing / Group Play |
| **Shadowhunting** | Spirit Animal Summons | Tactical Combat / Utility |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
Wards can be destroyed by enemies in PvP. Position your Healing Wards behind line-of-sight obstacles to ensure they continue ticking for your allies.
</Callout>
