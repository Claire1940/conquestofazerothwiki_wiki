<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Combo Breaker System">

The core of the **Conquest of Azeroth Templar** gameplay is the Combo Breaker. Unlike standard rotations where you press buttons on cooldown, the Templar requires precision. Using skills in the correct order unlocks enhanced effects that can turn the tide of a raid or PvP encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Energy">
    Start the encounter using basic martial strikes to build your energy reserves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initiate Sequence">
    Use your sequence-starting abilities (e.g., specific strikes) to prime the combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Finisher">
    Depending on the need (AOE vs Single Target), use the final skill in the sequence to trigger a Combo Breaker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Buff Maintenance">
    Many finishers provide temporary buffs like increased Dodge or Holy Damage; ensure these have high uptime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Combo Type | Sequence Goal | Resulting Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Vanguard Strike** | AOE Damage | Massive holy explosion around the Templar |
| **Defensive Flow** | Survivability | Increased Parry and Dodge for 10 seconds |
| **Righteous Fury** | Single Target | Increased Crit Damage on the next 3 strikes |
| **Healing Wave** | Utility | Small party-wide heal based on damage dealt |

<Callout type="warning" title="Sequence Sensitivity">
Missing a step in your combo sequence will fail to trigger the Combo Breaker, resulting in significantly lower DPS or threat generation. Practice your "rhythm" on training dummies.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Essential Templar Utility
Beyond combat, the Templar possesses several "quality of life" spells that make them excellent for world exploration and leveling:
- **Double Jump**: Reach high ledges and skip difficult terrain.
- **Water Walking**: Traverse lakes and oceans without mounts.
- **Resurrection**: Bring fallen allies back to the fight immediately.
- **Light Imbue**: Buff the party's movement speed and primary stats.

<Callout type="info" title="Exploration Pro">
Use the Double Jump in combination with Water Walking to navigate the expanded maps of Conquest of Azeroth much faster than other classes.
</Callout>
