<Section icon={Target} title="Inquisitor: The Path of Righteous Fire">

The Inquisitor is a relentless melee zealot that thrives on the duality of Light and Shadow. This spec requires mastering "Stance Dancing" to maximize damage. You cycle between **Purity** to build resources and **Wickedness** to unleash devastating fire and holy damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Purity Stance">
    Engage the enemy in **Purity Stance**. This increases your melee haste and allows you to build stacks of **Sensed Evil** through basic attacks and holy strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Switch to Wickedness">
    Once **Sensed Evil** is fully charged, switch to **Wickedness Stance**. This consumes your stacks to apply a vulnerability debuff to the target, increasing their damage taken from Holy and Fire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Flames of Sin">
    Use **Searing Blade** and **Smite Evil** while the target is vulnerable. This window provides the highest burst damage potential for the melee Witch Hunter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Stance | Primary Benefit | Key Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Purity** | **Haste Buff** / Resource Gen | Holy Blade Strikes |
| **Wickedness** | **Fire Damage** / Vulnerability | Flames of Sin |

<Callout type="success" title="Expert Strategy">
In group content, coordinate your **Wickedness** window with other Fire-based casters (like Pyromancers) to capitalize on the increased vulnerability debuff.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Black Knight: The Shadow-Infused Tank">

The Black Knight is the defensive pillar of the **conquest of azeroth witch hunter** class. By donning "Witchmail" and wielding a two-handed weapon, the Black Knight parries incoming blows while leeching the very life force from their attackers.

**Core Defensive Mechanics:**
- **Shadow Brand**: A persistent DoT that acts as your primary threat generator and healing source.
- **Desecrate**: Consumes Shadow Brands to restore a percentage of your maximum HP instantly.
- **Parry Mastery**: Unlike traditional shield tanks, the Black Knight relies on high parry chances with two-handed weapons.
- **Tonic Sustain**: Enhanced alchemical effects provide unique damage mitigation not found in other tank classes.

| Ability | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow Brand** | Debuff | Deals Shadow damage and marks the target for leeching. |
| **Desecrate** | Heal | Consumes brands to restore **Max HP**. |
| **Witchmail** | Passive | Increases armor and Shadow resistance significantly. |
| **Dark Justice** | Taunt | Forces the enemy to attack, applying a minor slow. |

<Callout type="info" title="Tanking Tip">
The Black Knight excels in **unparalleled sustain**. While you may take more raw damage than a Vanguard Guardian, your ability to heal through **Desecrate** makes you an "undying" avatar in long encounters.
</Callout>
