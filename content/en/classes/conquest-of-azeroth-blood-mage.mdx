<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Understanding the Blood Mage">

The Blood Mage is a unique, dark spellcaster introduced in Conquest of Azeroth. Unlike traditional casters who rely solely on mana, Blood Mages channel their own life force and build Rage to execute sinister designs. Whether you want to tank as a beast, heal through ritualistic damage, or deal devastating ranged damage, this class offers a highly interactive and aggressive playstyle.

**Video Highlights:**
- Blood Mages use Rage and health to cast abilities.
- The class is capable of fulfilling Tank, DPS, and Healer roles.
- Transformation mechanics are central to the Accursed and Eternal specializations.
- Life-leeching is the primary method for maintaining survival during combat.

<Section icon={Target} title="Progression and Mechanics">

Mastering the Blood Mage requires understanding the cycle of resources. You must learn when to spend your health and when to leech it back from enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Resource Management">
    Begin by understanding your Rage and HP interaction. Use abilities to build Rage while carefully monitoring your current health levels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering Transformations">
    For Accursed and Eternal specs, learn the timing for your transformations. Triggering these at the right moment can turn the tide of a difficult encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sustained Combat">
    Utilize your life-leeching spells like Vampiric Fang or Life Drinker to regain sanity and health, ensuring you do not succumb to your own dark powers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Health">
Entering the Hunger state or staying in a transformed form for too long can leave you vulnerable. Always have a leeching spell ready to recover from the health cost of your powerful attacks.
</Callout>

</Section>
