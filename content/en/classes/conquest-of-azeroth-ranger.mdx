<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Abilities & Skill Breakdown">

The Ranger's toolkit is divided into ranged archery, melee strikes, and utility skills that allow for excellent kiting and crowd control [DCKG674yIZ0]. Understanding how these abilities interact is key to maximizing your DPS rotation [DCKG674yIZ0].

### Ranged Archery Skills
*   **Quick Shot**: Your main builder. Generates 1 Archery Point per cast with a negligible cast time [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Skull Piercer Shot**: Your primary finisher. Consumes all Archery Points, increasing the base damage by 60% per point consumed [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Throat Cutter Shot**: An instant-cast interrupt with an 8-40 yard range [DCKG674yIZ0]. It prevents spellcasting for 3 seconds and reduces the target's spell haste by 30% [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Hail of Arrows**: A channeled AoE ability that can be cast while moving, though it reduces your movement speed by 80% while channeling [DCKG674yIZ0].

### Melee & Utility Skills
*   **Wild Strike**: An instant melee attack that increases your melee attack speed by 8% for 20 seconds, stacking up to 4 times (32% total bonus) [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Tracker's Leap**: A ground-targeted leap with a 5-second cooldown that increases your movement speed by 20% for 3 seconds [DCKG674yIZ0]. Excellent for kiting melee opponents [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Lure**: A utility stealth ability with a 10-second cooldown [DCKG674yIZ0]. It cannot be used to escape combat like a Rogue's Vanish, but it allows you to initiate fights from stealth at normal movement speed [DCKG674yIZ0].
*   **Horn of War**: A powerful 1-minute cooldown that increases your party and raid members' attack power by 25% and hit chance by 40% for 15 seconds [DCKG674yIZ0].

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Rotation">

To level your Ranger efficiently up to level 30 and beyond, follow this optimized combat loop to maintain high Focus recovery and maximize your Archery Point output [DCKG674yIZ0].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate from Stealth">
    Activate **Lure** before engaging a mob [DCKG674yIZ0]. Position yourself at maximum range (around 40 yards) to get the full benefit of your opening shots [DCKG674yIZ0].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Group Buffs">
    Blow your **Horn of War** to instantly boost your physical damage and hit rating before firing your first shot [DCKG674yIZ0].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Archery Points">
    Spam **Quick Shot** to build up to 5 Archery Points [DCKG674yIZ0]. Use **Throat Cutter Shot** instantly if the target begins casting a spell [DCKG674yIZ0].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash the Finisher">
    Fire **Skull Piercer Shot** at 5 Archery Points to execute the target with massive physical burst damage [DCKG674yIZ0].
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Kite or Transition to Melee">
    If the enemy survives and closes the distance, use **Tracker's Leap** to jump backward and reset your distance [DCKG674yIZ0]. Alternatively, swap to melee and stack **Wild Strike** to finish them off [DCKG674yIZ0].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Combo Point Persistence">
Remember that your Archery Points are stored on your character, not your target [DCKG674yIZ0]. If an enemy dies while you have 5 points, do not waste them; pull the next enemy and immediately open with a fully charged **Skull Piercer Shot** [DCKG674yIZ0].
