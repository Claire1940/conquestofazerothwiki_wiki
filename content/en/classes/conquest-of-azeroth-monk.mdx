<Section icon={Shield} title="The Warstaff Master: Ultimate Tanking Build">

The Warstaff Master is the definitive tanking build for the **conquest of azeroth monk**. This build leverages the Discipline talent tree to transform a staff-wielding martial artist into an impenetrable wall. The build's strength lies in its ability to stack high dodge chances and armor values, making it particularly effective against physical damage dealers in dungeons and raids.

The core of this build relies on specific talents that synergize with staff usage and defensive wards. Below is a breakdown of the essential talents for the Warstaff Master:

| Talent Name | Type | Effect Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Righteous Duel** | Aggro | Increases threat generation and damage during combat |
| **Transcendent Ward** | Shield | Provides a magical barrier that absorbs incoming damage |
| **Bastion** | Passive | Significantly boosts armor and block ratings |
| **Armor of Faith** | Defensive | Converts spirit or intellect into additional armor |
| **Rune Staff Slam** | Active | A high-impact strike that generates high threat |

While the build is exceptionally strong against physical threats, it requires careful management of cooldowns when facing magic-heavy encounters. Talents like **Grace of Aman'Thul** and **Infallible Ward** are often swapped in to assist with magic mitigation and the generation of "Broken Ward" stacks.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Martial and Crusader: DPS Alternatives">

If tanking isn't your primary goal, the **conquest of azeroth monk** excels as a melee damage dealer. The Martial spec focuses on raw physical speed, while the Crusader spec blends martial arts with holy magic to sever the spirits of enemies.

The Martial spec is built around the "Combat Moves" system. By utilizing fast-hitting fist weapons or daggers, you can generate combo points rapidly, leading into devastating finishing moves that can cleave through multiple targets.

| Spec Feature | Martial (Physical) | Crusader (Holy) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Stat** | Agility | Agility / Intellect |
| **Weapon Choice** | Fists / One-Handed | Swords / Staves |
| **Damage Type** | Physical / Bleed | Holy / Arcane |
| **Utility** | High Mobility | Spiritual Abdications |

The Crusader, on the other hand, is a graceful fighter. They use "Spiritual Abdications" to confuse enemies and deal holy damage in a shining vortex of blades. This spec is ideal for players who want to provide some utility to the raid while maintaining competitive DPS.
