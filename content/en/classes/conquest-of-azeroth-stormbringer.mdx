<Section icon={Map} title="Stormbringer Class Overview & Core Mechanics">

The **Conquest of Azeroth Stormbringer** represents the raw fury of the elements, acting as a master of thunderstorms and atmospheric pressure. Unlike traditional mages, the Stormbringer functions through a unique resource system known as **Static**. This resource is generated rapidly through standard spell casting but dissipates quickly if the player remains idle. Mastering the rhythm of building and spending Static is the difference between a novice and a high-tier elementalist.

The **Lightning** spec is the most straightforward, focusing on rapid-fire jolts and AOE electrification. **Maelstrom** introduces a more complex layer, requiring players to drench enemies with water spells to apply the **Conductive** debuff, which significantly amplifies the damage of subsequent lightning strikes. Finally, the **Wind** spec turns the Stormbringer into a defensive powerhouse, capable of pulling allies from danger and summoning Tailwinds to speed up the entire party.

| Specialization | Key Ability | Primary Debuff | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lightning** | Thunderous Eruption | Electrocuted | Maximum single-target DPS |
| **Maelstrom** | Frost Storm | Conductive | Massive AOE burst potential |
| **Wind** | Tailwinds | Gust/Pushback | Exceptional party utility |

<Callout type="warning" title="Maelstrom Complexity">
Maelstrom requires precise timing. If you detonate your Conductive stacks too early, you lose out on the exponential damage multiplier. Always aim for maximum stacks before unleashing your frost-storm finishers.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Rotation and Stat Priorities">

Optimizing your combat performance involves hitting specific stat thresholds and following a strict priority list. For the Stormbringer, **Haste** is the king of stats, as it directly correlates to how fast you can generate and spend Static.

**Primary Stat Priorities:**
1. **Intellect**: Increases overall spell power and mana pool.
2. **Haste**: Reduces cast times and speeds up Static generation.
3. **Critical Strike**: Vital for proc-based CD resets in the Lightning tree.
4. **Versatility**: Provides much-needed damage reduction for support builds.

**Standard DPS Rotation (Lightning/Maelstrom):**
- Apply **Conductive** stacks (if Maelstrom).
- Cast **Lightning Bolt** or **Shock** to build Static.
- Use **Living Lightning** on proc for free damage.
- Execute **Thunderous Eruption** at 90%+ Static.
- Use **Knockback** or **Root** if enemies close the gap.

| Stat | Priority | Target Goal (Level 60) | Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Haste** | High | 25% - 30% | Faster Static building |
| **Crit** | Medium | 20% | More CD resets |
| **Mastery** | High | 15% | Increased Elemental damage |

<Callout type="info" title="Support Rotation">
When playing as Wind support, your priority shifts to maintaining 100% uptime on **Tailwinds** and monitoring party mana levels. Use your air shields reactively rather than proactively to save mana.
</Callout>

</Section>
