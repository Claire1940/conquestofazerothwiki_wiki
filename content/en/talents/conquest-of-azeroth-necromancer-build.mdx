<Section icon={Map} title="Necromancer Class Overview and Core Identity">

The Necromancer in **Conquest of Azeroth** is a dedicated cloth-wearing spellcaster that redefines the classic summoner archetype. Unlike standard classes, the Necromancer utilizes a unique resource system known as **Life Force**. This resource is the lifeblood of your rotation, acting as both a fuel for your most devastating spells and the energy required to maintain a permanent army of the dead.

In the 2026 meta, the **Conquest of Azeroth Necromancer build** is primarily a DPS powerhouse. Whether you are spreading plagues or commanding a legion of skeletal archers, your success depends on balancing Life Force generation with high-impact spending. The class is designed for players who enjoy managing multiple units and maintaining high pressure through persistent magical effects.
