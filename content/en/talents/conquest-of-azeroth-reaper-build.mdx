<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Abilities and Skill Synergy">

To master the **conquest of azeroth reaper build**, you must understand the interaction between your basic strikes and your soul resource. Your primary rotation involves building **Reaped Souls**, which are generated by killing enemies or using specific skills like **Dirge** and **Reap**.

### Primary Combat Skills

The following table outlines the essential skills you will use during the leveling process and at the level 30 cap.

| Skill Name | Type | Key Effect | Resource Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reap** | Melee | Restores 5% Max Health; 3rd use grants a Reaped Soul | Mana / GCD |
| **Murder** | Ranged | Deals Shadow damage and restores 10% Max Mana | 10s Cooldown |
| **Soul Shock** | CC | Silences/Stuns target for 30s (breaks on damage) | 2s Cast |
| **Dirge** | Melee | Deals 220% weapon damage; generates one Reaped Soul | 5% Max Health |
| **Lament** | Ranged | Consumes one Reaped Soul for high Shadow damage | 5s Cooldown |

### The Soul Infusion State

Once you accumulate three **Reaped Souls**, you enter a state of **Soul Infusion**. This is the peak of the Reaper's power. For example, the ability **Shutter Scythe** normally delivers five attacks; however, under Soul Infusion, it delivers ten strikes over one second, effectively doubling its damage and healing potential.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Resource Management: Health and Souls">

The **conquest of azeroth reaper build** is a balancing act. You are constantly spending health to fuel your strongest attacks while using your passive abilities to regenerate mana and life.

### The Soul Harvester Passive
Whenever you deal direct damage, the enemy is marked for 10 seconds. If they die while marked, you and your allies gain 300% increased mana regeneration. Additionally, your maximum mana is permanently increased by 30%. This makes the Reaper an excellent partner in group play, acting as a "mana battery" for the team.

| Resource Type | Generation Method | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reaped Souls** | Killing mobs, Dirge, successful Parries | Consumed for Lament or Soul Infusion |
| **Mana** | Murder, Soul Harvester passive | Used for utility, CC, and range spells |
| **Health** | Reap, Blood Siphon, Shutter Scythe | Spent to use high-damage skills like Dirge |

<Callout type="warning" title="Health Sacrifice Risks">
Skills like **Dirge** cost 5% of your maximum health. In high-intensity PvP or boss fights, spamming these without a clear plan for health recovery can lead to self-inflicted death. Always pair Dirge with **Reap** to stabilize your HP.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Rotation">

To maximize your efficiency with a **conquest of azeroth reaper build**, follow this rotation to ensure you always have enough souls for your finishers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiation and Control">
    Start the fight with **Soul Shock** if the target is dangerous, followed by **Ghost Claw** to close the distance and steal their movement speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Soul Generation">
    Use **Dirge** (if healthy) to quickly gain Reaped Souls. Use **Reap** twice to build the combo, then use it a third time to generate your final soul.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Infusion Burst">
    Once you have three souls, activate **Shutter Scythe**. This will trigger the **Soul Infusion** effect, dealing massive damage and healing you back to full health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execution and Reset">
    Use **Murder** to regain mana and **Lament** to finish off any remaining health. If the target attempts to flee, use **Possessed** to close the gap and erupt for final shadow damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment and Stat Priority">

Reapers have flexible armor options, but for a standard **conquest of azeroth reaper build**, focusing on Agility and Stamina is paramount. Since your parry rating is boosted by 40% of your Agility while in **Jailer of Souls** mode, Agility serves as both an offensive and defensive stat.

| Equipment Slot | Recommended Type | Priority Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapons** | Dual-Wield One-Handed | Agility > Attack Power > Crit |
| **Armor** | Mail or Leather | Stamina > Agility > Haste |
| **Trinkets** | Shadow Damage / On-hit | Mana Regen > Strength |

### Weapon Requirements
- **Dual-Wielding**: Required for **Dirge**, **Underwalk**, and **Shift**.
- **Two-Handed Scythes**: Better for pure Reaping school focus and heavy single-hit damage.

<Callout type="info" title="Armor Selection">
While Reapers can wear Cloth, Leather, or Mail, **Mail** is highly recommended for the increased armor value, as you will often be in melee range taking physical hits while your health is already taxed by your own skills.
</Callout>
