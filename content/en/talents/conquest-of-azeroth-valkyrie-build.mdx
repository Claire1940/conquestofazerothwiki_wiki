<Section icon={Target} title="Valkyrie Rotation and Playstyle">

Mastering the rotation of a **conquest of azeroth valkyrie build** involves maintaining a high uptime on your Sunset buffs. Unlike traditional classes that follow a static priority list, the Valkyrie reacts to procs generated by Solar Power. Your goal is to reach 10 stacks of Sunset as quickly as possible to unleash a "Radiant Execution," which deals massive AoE damage and heals nearby allies for a percentage of the damage dealt.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Start with **Judgement of Fire** to apply a damage-over-time (DoT) effect and generate your first 2 Solar Power points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Building Momentum">
    Use **Sunstrike** on cooldown. This melee ability generates Sunset stacks and has a chance to reset the cooldown of your primary holy nuke.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resource Spending">
    Once at 50% Solar Power, activate **Aura of the Sun**. This drains your power but increases all holy damage by 25% for 10 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    At 10 Sunset stacks, cast **Heavenly Smite**. This consumes all stacks to guarantee a critical strike and provides a shielding effect to the player.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Overcap">
Avoid sitting at maximum Solar Power. Any power generated beyond the cap is wasted, significantly lowering your overall DPS and healing throughput in long encounters.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="PvE and PvP Performance Comparison">

The **conquest of azeroth valkyrie build** excels in different ways depending on the game mode. In PvE, the Valkyrie is a "Utility King," providing essential buffs like **Blessing of the Sun** to the entire raid while maintaining competitive DPS. In PvP, particularly High Risk scenarios, the Valkyrie's mobility (via **Valkyrie Wings**) and burst healing make it a nightmare for assassins like the Reaper or Ranger to take down.

However, the build does have weaknesses. It relies heavily on staying within melee range to generate Sunset stacks. If a kiting class, such as a Stormbringer or Chronomancer, can keep the Valkyrie at a distance, the spec's effectiveness drops significantly.

| Mode | Strength | Weakness | 2026 Tier |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Dungeons** | Excellent AoE and off-healing | High mana consumption | **S-Tier** |
| **Raiding** | Strong raid-wide buffs | Complex rotation | **A-Tier** |
| **Arena** | High burst and survivability | Vulnerable to silences | **A-Tier** |
| **High Risk** | Great escape tools and tankiness | Gear dependent | **S-Tier** |

<Callout type="tip" title="PvP Counter-Play">
When facing kiting classes in PvP, save your **Valkyrie Wings** for the moment they use their displacement ability (like Blink). Using it too early leaves you stranded.
</Callout>
