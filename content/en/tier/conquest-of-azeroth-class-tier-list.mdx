<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start Your Journey on Vol'Jin">

Starting your adventure in Conquest of Azeroth requires a different approach than standard WoW. Follow these steps to ensure your class choice aligns with your goals for the 2026 season.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Download & Realm Selection">
    Install the official **Project Ascension launcher**. Ensure you select the **Vol'Jin** realm, which is the dedicated home for the Conquest of Azeroth 21-class experience.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Character Creation">
    Pick one of the 21 original classes. Refer to the **conquest of azeroth class tier list** above if you are undecided. Remember, your class choice is permanent for that character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Your Trainer">
    At level 3, you will receive a prompt to find your **Class Trainer**. These NPCs are located in starting zones like Goldshire or Brill and are essential for unlocking your core kit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spec Selection at Level 10">
    This is the most critical milestone. Choose your primary specialization (e.g., Tinker Demolition vs. Mechanics). This unlocks your Dragonflight-style talent trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Enable Auto-Build (Optional)">
    If the 1000+ spells and talents feel overwhelming, toggle the **Auto-Build** option in the talent menu to have the game allocate points for you until you learn the rotation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Trainer Locations">
Class trainers have been added to all major starting hubs. If you are lost, check the **Barrens** or **Westfall** hubs, as they serve as secondary training grounds for mid-level progression.
</Callout>
