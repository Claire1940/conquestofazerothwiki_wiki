<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Optimize Your DPS Build">

Maximizing your position on the **conquest of azeroth dps tier list** requires a deep understanding of the talent system. Every class features a Class Tree and a Specialization Tree. Points alternate as you level, meaning your build evolves gradually from level 1 to 60.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Specialization at Level 10">
    This is the most critical decision. Your spec determines your primary spells and which talent tree you will invest in alongside your Class Tree.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Auto-Build for Leveling">
    If you are new to the 21-class system, enable Auto-Build. It will automatically allocate points into proven paths, ensuring you stay competitive while learning the class mechanics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus on Primary Stat Scaling">
    Identify whether your spec scales with Intellect, Strength, or Agility. In CoA, gear often features "Immersive Loot" properties that provide class-specific bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Your Resource Loop">
    Whether it's managing "Rounds" as a Tinker or "Deathfire" as a Knight of Xoroth, your DPS is tied to how efficiently you generate and spend your unique class resource.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Respecing for Endgame">
Once you reach level 60, visit your Class Trainer to respec manually. The optimal leveling build often differs from the best raid build, which requires more focus on single-target efficiency and threat reduction.
</Callout>
