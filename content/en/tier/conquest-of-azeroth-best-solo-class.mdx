<Section icon={Target} title="Steps to Configure Your Solo Build">

Setting up your solo character requires a clear understanding of the game's custom progression systems. Unlike standard World of Warcraft, Conquest of Azeroth utilizes a Dragonflight-style talent tree split into a Class Tree and a Specialization Tree [1]. Follow these steps to properly configure your character for optimal solo performance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Your Class Trainer Early">
    Your journey begins in the starting zones [1]. Watch for the level 3 class quest prompt, which directs you to your local class trainer in areas like Goldshire, Kharanos, or Brill [1]. Learning your base class kit early is vital for establishing your starting rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Your Specialization at Level 10">
    Upon reaching level 10, open your talent interface and select your primary specialization [1]. For solo play, choose specs that offer pets or high self-healing (such as Animation for Necromancer or Sanguine for Bloodmage) [1]. This choice unlocks your second talent tree.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Leverage the Auto-Build System">
    If the massive talent trees feel overwhelming, enable the built-in Auto-Build option [1]. This system automatically allocates your talent points into optimized, developer-approved paths, ensuring your solo build remains competitive without manual math.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize Gear with Immersive Stats">
    Conquest of Azeroth features completely rebuilt world systems, including unique RPG creature stats and immersive gear drops [1]. As a solo player, prioritize Stamina alongside your primary stat (Intellect for casters, Strength or Agility for physical classes) to survive unexpected open-world ambushes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Respec Customization">
Do not worry about making mistakes early on. You can easily reset your talent points at your class trainer [1]. If you find your current solo spec feels too slow, you can swap to a faster, high-burst damage build once you acquire better gear.
</Callout>
