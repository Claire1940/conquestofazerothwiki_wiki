<Section icon={Target} title="How to Setup Mobile Gameplay">

Setting up your device for **conquest of azeroth mobile** requires a few specific steps to ensure the custom Ascension launcher features function correctly. Since CoA shares the same client as Ascension Classless, the setup process is unified.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Launcher Preparation">
    Ensure you have the latest Ascension Launcher installed on your host PC. Select the 'Conquest of Azeroth' realm from the dropdown menu to download the specific 2026 Alpha patches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Streaming Software Configuration">
    Install Moonlight (for NVIDIA/AMD users) or Steam Link on your mobile device. Configure the host PC to allow remote connections and add the Ascension Executable to your library.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="UI & Addon Setup">
    Install the **ConsolePort** addon within the CoA directory. This is essential for mobile play as it converts the standard WoW interface into a controller-friendly radial menu system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Input Mapping">
    Connect a Bluetooth controller (Xbox, PS5, or Backbone) to your phone. Map your primary class mechanics—like the Necromancer's Life Force or the Sun Cleric's Solar Power—to easily accessible triggers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Ready for Launch">
Once your controller is synced, launch the game. The ConsolePort addon will automatically detect your device and provide a setup wizard to help you bind your new custom spells.
</Callout>
