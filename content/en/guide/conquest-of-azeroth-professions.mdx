<Section icon={BookOpen} title="New Professions: Woodworking and Woodcutting">

The 2026 expansion introduces **Woodworking** as a primary crafting profession and **Woodcutting** as its gathering counterpart. These were designed to fill the gaps left by traditional professions, specifically providing a reliable source for ranged weapons, magical implements, and shields.

**Woodworking Key Features:**
- **Weapon Variety**: Craft stabs, wands, bows, crossbows, and throwing weapons.
- **Defensive Gear**: Create specialized shields and off-hand items.
- **Ammunition**: Produce high-quality arrows and bolts with unique properties.
- **Random Affixes**: Every crafted piece can roll specific stats like Armor Penetration for Witch Hunters or Spell Power for Tinkers.

| Craftable Item | Primary Stats | Target Classes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reinforced Bows** | Agility, Crit, Haste | Ranger, Star Caller, Witch Hunter |
| **Etched Staves** | Intellect, Spirit, Haste | Chronomancer, Necromancer, Pyromancer |
| **Heavy Shields** | Strength, Block, Parry | Guardian, Templar, Sun Cleric |
| **Throwing Axes** | Strength, Attack Power | Barbarian (Headhunting Spec) |
| **Arcane Wands** | Intellect, Spell Power | Stormbringer, Blood Mage, Cultist |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Woodcutting">
    Visit a profession trainer in any major starting zone to learn Woodcutting. This allows you to track and harvest timber nodes across Azeroth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Raw Timber">
    Explore forests like Elwynn or Shadowglen. Higher-level zones yield rarer woods required for Blue and Epic quality Woodworking recipes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Refine and Craft">
    Use a Woodworking bench (found in cities or via class utility) to transform raw timber into weapons and shields. Use **Upgrade Kits** to keep your favorite crafts relevant as you level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management">
Wood and timber can take up significant bag space. It is highly recommended to invest in large profession bags or use a dedicated bank alt to manage your stockpiles.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Crafting Mechanics and Gear Upgrades">

Crafting in the **conquest of azeroth professions** system is not just about making an item once. It is a continuous process of refinement. The 2026 update emphasizes the use of **Upgrade Kits** and **Runes of Ascension** to ensure that professions remain relevant from level 1 to the highest tiers of Mythic+ dungeons and Ascended raids.

**Key Crafting Systems:**
- **Upgrade Kits**: These allow you to increase the item level and stats of a crafted piece multiple times, preventing gear from becoming obsolete every few levels.
- **Random Affixes**: Crafted gear can roll "hybrid" stats that are normally rare, such as plate armor with Intellect or daggers with Strength.
- **Worldforged Integration**: You can combine profession-crafted items with Worldforged effects to create truly unique character builds.

| Upgrade Stage | Required Materials | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Upgrade** | Standard profession mats | Small stat boost, +5 Item Level |
| **Advanced Upgrade** | Rare gathered mats | Significant stat boost, +10 Item Level |
| **Masterwork** | Dungeon/Raid drops | Unlocks a secondary random affix slot |
| **Ascended** | Runes of Ascension | Scales item to current endgame tier |

<Callout type="info" title="Random Affix Potential">
A Blacksmithing Knight of Zoroth can forge an Arcanite Reaper with both **Strength and Spell Power**, perfectly complementing their hybrid melee-caster playstyle. Similarly, a Woodworking Tinker can craft a gun with **Intellect and Crit**.
</Callout>

For players seeking a greater challenge, professions play a critical role in the **Hardcore Trials** and **High-Risk PvP** environments. In these modes, the ability to self-sustain through crafting is often the difference between progression and character loss.

**Lonely Artisan Trial:**
In this specific hardcore challenge, you only gain experience through crafting and gathering. This turns the game into a pure RPG tycoon experience where you must master the economy and resource routes of Azeroth to reach the level cap.

**High-Risk Survival:**
In High-Risk PvP, you drop loot upon death. Having high-level professions allows you to quickly replace lost gear without relying on expensive Auction House prices or dangerous dungeon runs.
