<Section icon={Swords} title="All Heirloom Sets & Stat Distributions">

Conquest of Azeroth features specialized heirloom sets tailored to different class archetypes. Because armor types (such as Mail and Plate) shift at level 40, these sets dynamically upgrade their armor values to match your class requirements.

### Champion’s Battlegear (Physical DPS)
Designed for physical damage dealers such as Hunters or physical-scaling custom specs.
- **Key Stats**: Agility, Attack Power, Haste, Hit Rating, Intellect, Stamina.
- **Armor Scaling**: Functions as Leather from levels 1–39, automatically upgrades to Mail at level 40.

### Stained Shadowcraft (Agility/Melee DPS)
Optimized for high-mobility melee specs.
- **Key Stats**: Agility, Attack Power, Critical Strike, Haste, Hit, Stamina.
- **Armor Type**: Leather.

### Mystical Elements (Caster/Healer Hybrid)
Best suited for elemental and restoration hybrid specs.
- **Key Stats**: Intellect, Spell Power, Haste, Mp5, Stamina.
- **Armor Scaling**: Functions as Leather from levels 1–39, automatically upgrades to Mail at level 40.

### Battlegear of Valor (Strength DPS)
Designed for frontline plate-wearing damage specs.
- **Key Stats**: Strength, Critical Strike, Haste, Hit, Stamina.
- **Armor Scaling**: Functions as Mail from levels 1–39, automatically upgrades to Plate at level 40.

### Tattered Dreadmist (Spell DPS/Cloth)
The premier choice for pure casters.
- **Key Stats**: Spell Power, Intellect, Haste, Hit, Spirit, Stamina.
- **Armor Type**: Cloth.

### Burnished Armor of Might (Tanking)
The definitive set for frontline tanks.
- **Key Stats**: Strength, Block Value, Defense, Dodge, Hit, Stamina.
- **Armor Scaling**: Functions as Mail from levels 1–39, automatically upgrades to Plate at level 40.

### Preened Ironfeather (Caster/Druid)
Excellent for mana-based caster classes.
- **Key Stats**: Spell Power, Intellect, Critical Strike, Spirit, Stamina.
- **Armor Type**: Leather.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Heirloom Speed Leveling Setup">

To maximize your leveling speed in Conquest of Azeroth, follow this optimized setup process.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Your First Level 60">
    Complete your first normal leveling run to level 60 on your home realm to lift the heirloom restriction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Runes and Tokens">
    Farm daily callboards and scaling dungeons on your main character to hoard Runes of Ascension and Tokens of Prestige.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase the Core XP Pieces">
    Prioritize buying the Chest, Shoulders, Cloak, and Weapons first, as these are the specific slots that grant raw experience bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Low-Level Scaling Enchants">
    Apply powerful enchants to your heirlooms. Use stat enchants on your chest, cloaks, and weapons to make early-game combat trivial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reclaim on Your Alts">
    Log into your new custom class alt, open the Vanity Tab, and click 'Deliver' to instantly equip your fully enchanted, scaling gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>
