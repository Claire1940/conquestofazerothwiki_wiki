<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Hybrid Risk PvP System">

The core of **conquest of azeroth pvp** is built upon a flexible architecture that accommodates various player preferences. Unlike traditional servers that lock you into a specific realm type, this project utilizes a "Hybrid Risk" system. This allows all players to exist in the same world phase while choosing the level of danger they are comfortable with.

| Mode | PvP Interaction | Loot Rules | Special Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PvE Mode** | None (Untargetable) | Standard | Full access to grouping and raids |
| **PvP Mode** | Open World Combat | Standard | Standard honor and prestige gains |
| **High Risk** | Full Combat (Separate Phase) | **Item Drop on Death** | Access to Bloodforged items and rare mats |

In High Risk PvP, you are classified as a Treasure Hunter. This specific status grants a powerful buff that enables monsters in the open world to drop Bloodforged versions of Dungeon and Raid gear. This means you can effectively gear up for high-level combat entirely through open-world participation, provided you can survive the player-driven threats.

<Callout type="warning" title="High Risk Warning">
In High Risk mode, slaying a hero allows you to loot a chest containing pieces of their equipped gear and inventory. Always carry backup sets if you intend to venture into contested territories.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Gearing for PvP: The Bloodforged Path">

Gearing in **conquest of azeroth pvp** differs significantly from retail WoW. While you can run dungeons and raids for standard gear, the most efficient path for a dedicated PvP player is the High Risk open world.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable High Risk Mode">
    Switch to the High Risk phase via the realm selector. Ensure you have a basic set of gear that you are willing to lose.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Treasure Hunter">
    Once in the High Risk phase, you receive the Treasure Hunter buff. This increases your luck and enables unique drops from all world mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Contested Zones">
    Target high-density areas in Kalimdor or the Eastern Kingdoms. Slay monsters to find **Bloodforged gear**, which features upgraded stats compared to standard versions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extract and Trade">
    Bloodforged gear is non-soulbound. If you find a piece that doesn't fit your class, you can trade it on the Auction House for gold to buy the pieces you need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Bloodforged items are the pinnacle of PvP equipment. They often bridge the gap between casual players and raid-tier veterans, allowing skill and risk-taking to dictate power levels rather than just time spent in instances.

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Zerg Mechanics">
High Risk zones often have mechanics to discourage massive groups from camping single players. Stay mobile and avoid staying in one sub-zone for too long to minimize your visibility on the map.
</Callout>

</Section>
