<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Runemaster Engravement Leveling Build">

The Runemaster's Engravement build is highly recommended for post-wipe recovery. This spec functions similarly to a rogue but bypasses the tedious management of energy or combo points. Instead, it relies on mana and powerful weapon engravings that trigger powerful magical effects on every strike.

### Weapon Engravings Priority

Your choice of weapon engravings determines your overall damage profile. Fast weapons are highly recommended because more attacks translate directly to more engraving procs.

| Hand | Engraving | Best For | Proc Chance & Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Main Hand | Fire | All Situations | 30% chance to deal high fire damage |
| Off-Hand (AoE) | Earth | Dungeons & Cleave | 15% chance to deal uncapped AoE damage |
| Off-Hand (Single) | Ice | Bosses & PvP | 40% chance to apply 65 frost damage |

### Key Talents & Leveling Priority

Focus your early talent points on maximizing your mana regeneration and extending your burst windows.

| Talent Name | Minimum Level | Core Function | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Runic Tattoos (Water) | Level 10 | Increases Water Tattoo mana regen by 200% | High Priority |
| Zenith Extension | Level 20 | Increases Zenith duration by 6 seconds | Mandatory |
| Warp Dagger Buff | Level 30 | Increases magic damage by 10% from stealth | Recommended |

<Callout type="success" title="The Double Fire Trick">
Early on, before unlocking the Ice or Earth engravings, you can trick the system to run Fire on both weapons. Place your main-hand weapon in your bag, apply the Fire engraving to your off-hand, and then re-equip your main hand. This substantially boosts your early-game DPS.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup & Starting Zone Rush">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Runic Tattoos (Water)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set Up Your Zenith Macro">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Dungeon Cleaving">

  </Step>
