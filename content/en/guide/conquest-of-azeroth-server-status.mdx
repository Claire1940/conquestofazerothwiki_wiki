<Section icon={Shield} title="How to verify availability without wasting time">

The cleanest approach is to follow the same verification order every time. That keeps you from chasing the wrong problem. If the realm is not open, client repair will not help. If the realm is open, repeated relogs will not solve a broken addon stack or a stale cache.

A good routine is to check status, confirm the client version, strip away obvious conflicts, and then test the connection again. Use a calm pace. Launch nights reward players who verify once, adjust once, and then wait for the next stable window.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the status label">
    Confirm whether the realm is marked online, offline, or upcoming before you change anything in your client.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Match the label to the problem">
    If the realm is offline, do not troubleshoot your PC first. If it is online, move to client-side checks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Test a clean login path">

  <Step num="4" title="Retry on a short timer">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Common login problems and the right fix">

When the status looks fine but the login still fails, treat the issue as a triage problem. The goal is not to touch every setting. The goal is to isolate the cause in the fewest steps possible. In practice, that means starting with the client, then moving to your network, then looking at the realm again.

This is also where players waste the most time. A disconnect at character select, a stalled “connecting” screen, and a login timeout often share the same root pattern: one layer is failing before the next layer can finish loading. Keep your response simple and systematic.

<Callout type="info" title="Fix the Local Variables First">
If the realm is online but the client is failing, assume your setup is the problem until the clean test proves otherwise.
</Callout>

| Symptom | Likely cause | Best fix |
|---|---|---|
| Stuck on connecting | Traffic spike or handshake delay | Restart once, then wait a few minutes |
| Instant disconnect | Version mismatch or addon conflict | Repair the client and disable addons |
| Character select error | Realm load pressure | Recheck status and try again later |
| Login works, then lags | Peak-hour congestion | Stay logged in and avoid relogs |
