<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Strategy">

Leveling in Conquest of Azeroth requires a mixture of custom questing, open-world exploration, and tactical matchmaking. Follow this structured progression path to optimize your journey to level 60.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Utilize Starting Travel Permits">
    Use your free travel permit (available up to level 8) to instantly teleport to any starting zone that is less crowded [PICYx25zl4A]. This avoids spawn bottlenecks during high-population launch windows [PICYx25zl4A].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Focus on Custom Zone Quests">
    Complete the newly added custom quests in classic zones like Westfall [PICYx25zl4A]. These quests offer significantly higher experience rates and drop powerful Worldforged items [PICYx25zl4A].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Your Specialization at Level 10">
    Upon reaching level 10, immediately open your talent menu to select your specialization [PICYx25zl4A]. This unlocks your signature class mechanics, such as the Venomancer's Beetle Form or Rotweaver abilities [PICYx25zl4A].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Custom Leveling Zones">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Hybrid Risk System Explained">

The server features an integrated risk framework designed to accommodate both casual PvE players and hardcore PvP enthusiasts on a single, unified realm [332].

| Game Mode | Open-World Rules | Unique Rewards & Mechanics |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **PvE Mode** | Safe from enemy players | Standard questing, dungeons, and raids [332] |
| **PvP Mode** | Open-world faction combat | Classic honor generation, no item loss [332] |
| **High Risk PvP** | Full-loot open-world combat | Defeated players drop equipped and inventory items [332] |

### High-Risk Treasure Hunting

While flagged for High-Risk PvP, players receive the **Treasure Hunter** buff [332]. This allows you to find highly coveted **Bloodforged** variations of dungeon and raid equipment directly from normal monsters in the open world [332]. Because these items are non-soulbound, they form the cornerstone of the server's trade economy [332].

<Callout type="warning" title="High-Risk Warning">
Do not enter High-Risk PvP zones with gear you cannot afford to lose [332]. Keep your high-value progression gear in your bank until you are confident in your class mechanics and escape options [PICYx25zl4A].
</Callout>
