### PvP: Risk and Reward
CoA offers three distinct world phases to cater to different player preferences. You can switch between these modes in major cities.

- **PvE Mode**: Safe exploration. No open-world combat unless flagged.
- **PvP Mode**: Standard open-world combat. No item loss.
- **High Risk Phase**: A separate layer where defeated players drop gear chests. This mode offers **Bloodforged** loot, which is more powerful than standard drops.

<Callout type="success" title="High Risk Rewards">
Engaging in High Risk PvP is the fastest way to obtain tradeable gear. Even if you don't fight other players, killing monsters in this phase grants access to the Bloodforged loot table, providing a significant power boost.
</Callout>
