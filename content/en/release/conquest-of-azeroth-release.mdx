<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Modes and High Risk Systems">

One of the defining features of the **conquest of azeroth release** is the Hybrid Risk system. Players are not forced into one style of play; instead, you can toggle your world phase to match your preferred level of danger.

| Mode | Risk Level | Ruleset | Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PvE Mode** | **Low** | No open-world PvP | Safe leveling & exploration |
| **PvP Mode** | **Medium** | Open-world combat enabled | PvP currency & World conflict |
| **High Risk** | **High** | Drop gear on death | **Bloodforged** gear & rare loot |

**High Risk PvP** is a separate phase where the stakes are highest. If you are defeated by another player, you may drop a chest containing pieces of your equipped gear. However, the monsters in this phase drop significantly better loot, including "Bloodforged" items that are otherwise unavailable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Phase">
    Select your risk mode at any major city. Note that you can only group with players in the same phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip 'Risk-Ready' Gear">
    In High Risk, do not wear anything you aren't prepared to lose. Use "Crow's Cache" items or crafted gear for these sessions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt for Bloodforged Items">
    Focus on elite areas in the open world. These mobs have a high chance to drop tradeable, powerful gear in the High Risk phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stealth Restrictions">
In Conquest of Azeroth, stealth uptime is heavily restricted. Unless you are playing a specific specialization like the **Ranger (Brigand)** or **Venomancer (Stalking)**, you cannot remain invisible indefinitely. This prevents "stealth-ganking" from dominating the High Risk phase.
</Callout>

</Section>
