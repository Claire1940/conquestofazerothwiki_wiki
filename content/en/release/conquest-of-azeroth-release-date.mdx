The progression system utilizes a dual-tree structure inspired by modern RPG design. Upon reaching level 10, you unlock the ability to specialize, which fundamentally alters how your class interacts with the world.

<Section icon={Swords} title="PvE, PvP, and High Risk Modes">

The world is divided into different "Phases" or modes that dictate the level of danger and reward you encounter. This system ensures that both hardcore competitive players and casual explorers can enjoy the **Conquest of Azeroth release date** content on their own terms.

**Available Gameplay Modes:**
1. **PvE Mode**: Open-world PvP is disabled. Focus on questing, dungeons, and raids without interference.
2. **PvP Mode**: Full open-world combat is enabled. Fight for territory and pride without losing your gear.
3. **High Risk PvP**: A separate phase where defeated players drop chests containing a portion of their inventory and equipped items.
