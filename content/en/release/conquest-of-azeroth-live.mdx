### Top Starter Class Spotlights

- **Necromancer (S-Tier)**: Extremely popular due to its high safety during solo leveling. It utilizes a unique Life Force resource to summon undead minions, cast chilling frost magic, and spread devastating plagues.
- **Bloodmage (S-Tier)**: A highly versatile hybrid class that can tank, heal, or deal damage. It spends health to power high-impact blood spells and features incredible self-sustain through vampiric lifesteal.
- **Reaper (S-Tier)**: A plate-wearing shadow knight that harvests the souls of fallen enemies. Reapers make fantastic starter tanks and solo bruisers because their melee attacks constantly heal them.
- **Guardian (S-Tier)**: The ultimate group protector. Armed with plate armor, a sturdy shield, and inspiring banners, they can easily hold threat as a tank or provide critical stat buffs to their party as a support.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Modernized Talent Trees & Builds">

Conquest of Azeroth overhauls the classic talent system in favor of a dual-tree design inspired by modern retail expansions. Upon reaching level 10, players unlock their primary specialization. From there, you will spend talent points across two separate trees: a unified Class Tree that offers utility common to all specs of your class, and a Specialization Tree that refines your specific combat role.

Talent points alternate between the Class Tree and the Specialization Tree as you level up, ensuring your character gains both utility and raw power at a steady pace.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Overhauled PvE Encounters">

The PvE endgame in Conquest of Azeroth is built to challenge the unique utility and support capabilities of the 21 custom classes. Dungeons are fully integrated into a scaling difficulty system, allowing coordinated groups to progress from entry-level Normal runs all the way to high-intensity Mythic+ keys.

| PvE Difficulty | Party Size | Mechanics & Tuning | Gearing Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal** | 5 Players | Baseline Vanilla routes, ideal for leveling | Leveling rare items |
| **Heroic** | 5 Players | Increased health pools and damage output | Level 60 starter gear |
| **Mythic** | 5 Players | Added boss mechanics and interrupt checks | Epic dungeon progression |
| **Mythic+** | 5 Players | Scaling health, damage, and custom affixes | Repeatable endgame loot |
| **Flex Raids** | 10-25 Players | Redesigned classic encounters (Molten Core) | Top-tier raid epics |

In addition to dungeons, raids have been overhauled to support flexible group sizes ranging from 10 to 25 players. Iconic encounters like Molten Core and Onyxia's Lair feature modernized boss mechanics, custom phases, and specialized loot tables designed specifically for the new classes. 

The world itself feels more like a traditional RPG due to the **RPG Creature Overhaul**. Open-world monsters now possess custom stats, and they drop the exact armor and weapons they are wearing when defeated.

<Callout type="info" title="Redesigned Raid Mechanics">
You cannot rely on classic Vanilla strategy guides for raids on this server. Bosses have been fully updated with new spell interrupts, tank-swap mechanics, and raid-wide damage phases that require active coordination and support-role utility.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="PvP Modes and High Risk Phases">

Conquest of Azeroth features a flexible, toggleable ruleset system that allows players of all playstyles to coexist on the same server. By opening the activities menu in any major sanctuary city, players can toggle between three distinct open-world phases.

| PvP Mode | Risk Profile | Open-World Rules | Unique Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PvE Mode** | Zero Risk | Safe from enemy player attacks | Peaceful questing and gathering |
| **PvP Mode** | Medium Risk | Faction-based open-world combat | Honor points and world PvP combat |
| **High Risk** | Extreme Risk | Separate phase with full-loot rules | Bloodforged gear drops |

For competitive players, the endgame features structured battlegrounds and rated arenas in 1v1, 2v2, and 3v3 formats. Additionally, custom world events occur regularly:
- **Crow's Cache**: A high-stakes guild-vs-guild battle in the open world over a locked chest of epic spoils.
- **Gurubashi Blood Bowl**: A free-for-all gladiatorial arena event where players compete for killing streaks and exclusive cosmetic rewards.

<Callout type="warning" title="High Risk Full-Loot Rules">
When entering the High Risk phase, defeating an enemy player allows you to loot a chest containing pieces of their equipped gear and inventory. Likewise, if you die, you will lose your items. Only venture into this phase with gear you can easily replace!
</Callout>
