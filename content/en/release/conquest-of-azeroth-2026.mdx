<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Talent Trees and Build Customization">

The talent system is the core of your character's progression. Drawing heavy inspiration from modern talent architectures, each class features a split tree system consisting of a **Class Tree** and a **Specialization Tree**. As you level up, you will alternate earning points for both trees, allowing you to customize your utility, defensive capabilities, and offensive multipliers simultaneously.

At level 10, choosing your specialization locks in your primary playstyle. However, your Class Tree remains shared across all specs, meaning a healing-focused Pyromancer can still access powerful fire-shielding utility from the shared class nodes.
